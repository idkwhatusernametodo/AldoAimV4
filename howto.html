How to use

1

Prepare Your Executor

Make sure you have a supported executor installed. We recommend Xeno, Wave, or Bunni.

⚠️ Note: Most paid executors might not work as well since I have no way to test them.
2

Copy the Script

Click the copy button below to copy the latest loadstring.

loadstring(game:HttpGet("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/idkwhatusernametodo/AldoAims/refs/heads/main/Arsenal.lua"))()
3

Inject & Execute

1. Open Roblox and join a game.
2. Click the Inject (or Attach) button on your executor.
3. Paste the script into the text box and click Execute.
